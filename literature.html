Poetry

In my first year of secondary school I wrote some poetry, as poetry is one of my hobbies.
The two poems I wrote were:
  • Oh Creation!,
  • MAN



Oh Creation!

Oh creation, with the birds and the fishes,
Oh creation, granting all my wishes;
Oh creation, with the rainbow in the sky,
When I gaze, I must say, 'Oh my'.

Oh creation, with the rain and the sun,
Oh creation, filling me with joy and fun;
And oh, plants, with their beautiful flowers,
Beautiful enough to be thought to have powers.

And wow, us humans, with our marvelous brains,
For us to know our rejoices and pains;
Can creation be more beauttiful, I wonder;
All I know is it's as beautiful as ever.

Man

Man started well with perfection and paradise,
But deceit and fallacies overtook his selfish, naive mind;
His love for God and faith in Him was left behind.

He went on to live in suffering and sin,
To fight a battle impossible to win;
Things for him continue to get worse every second,
All he can do now is ponder over how his life horribly turned.

At the end, he will be risen up once again,
He will be freed from this prison chain;
He will enjoy happiness and joy everyday;
He will have a second chance to follow the right way.
